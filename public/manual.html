User Manual

You can directly open scanner on your Android device to get redirected to your feedback form website or you can use the following url to reach your destination web-page

The first thing than is to "Sign in" for identification process

Here, you can use your mobile number to verify the identity using OTP sent on your registered number

Now you can set your password for future verification process

Once you complete the verification process and setting up password your account is created

Simply you can login to this web-application using your Mobile number and password

Now you can fill the feedback form and put your honest response of experince with police service provided to you.

And just submit your response using "Submit" button

You can also check the information about the respective police station by going to "police station information"
